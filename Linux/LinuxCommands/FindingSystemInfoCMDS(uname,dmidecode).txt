->FINDING SYSTEM INFORMATION
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------
	.cat /etc/redhat-release
	------------------------------------------------------------
	- This will give the information about operating system.

	.uname -a
	-------------------------------------------------------------
	- This will also give you information about operating system
	kernal.
	

	.dmidecode (change to root)
	-------------------------------------------------------------
	- This will also give you information about operating system, 
	processor, memory, hardware etc
